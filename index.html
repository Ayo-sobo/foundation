About Us

Drsavealife Foundation is an organization established to realize the vision 2025 of Dr. Richard Okoye for Nigeria which is, increasing the life expectancy of Nigerians from 52 to 70 years. With strong Christian values and heritage, Drsavealife Foundation is committed to spearheading the drive to improve the quality of healthcare both in our local communities and urban areas with the highest level of clinical excellence, along with a strong organizational culture that places priority and resources on the need for accessible quality healthcare delivery. If you share our values and ambition about increasing the life expectancy of Nigerians, we ask that you get involved with us by applying, volunteering, or simply spreading the word about what we do. Join our Journey!

Our Vision

We envision a Nigeria where life expectancy is as high as 70 years. Where people have access to world class medical services at affordable rate

*

Our Mission

To provide truthful, informed, balanced, medically sound, research-based information and cutting-edge updates on prevention and treatment of all medical conditions, and infectious diseases, that affect health, quality of life and longevity of Africans.
